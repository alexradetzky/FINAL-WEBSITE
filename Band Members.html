Let's Meet the Band!

Trey Anastasio Mike Gordon Page McConnell Jon Fishman

Phish is comprised of guitarist/vocalist Trey Anastasio, bassist Mike Gordon, Pianist Page McConnell, and drummer Jon Fishman. These four have been consistently playing together for nearly 22 years. The chemistry between the group has long been attributed to their success as an improvisational jam band.

"To their fans, Phish are not just a band, they're a way of life. Although the group's studio albums sell well enough, Phish are primarily a live phenomenon: the living, breathing, noodling embodiment of the term 'jam band.' Like Deadheads before them, Phish fans have followed the group city to city, traded bootleg tapes with the band's blessings, and feverishly debate the merits of past gigs (Phish never plays the same set twice). By nurturing this grass-roots following, Phish bypassed commercial radio and evolved over the course of a decade into one of—if not the— hottest live attractions in America." -Rolling Stone
